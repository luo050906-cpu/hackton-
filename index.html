Weapons of Mass Distraction

Control the Scroll is a project that aims to raise awareness about the addictive nature of social media platforms and the impact they have on our mental health. The project consists of a website that simulates the experience of being trapped in a social media feed, with fake notifications, time loading, and a trap screen. The goal of the project is to encourage users to take control of their social media usage and to be more mindful of the content they consume.
