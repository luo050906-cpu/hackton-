Our Project: Escape The Scroll

Escape The Scroll is not just another social media clone. It is a psychological experience designed to make users feel the addictive nature of endless scrolling - and then guide them toward breaking free.

Our Vision

Instead of copying platforms like Instagram or TikTok, we created a digital trap that simulates how these apps capture attention.

We want users to:

  • Realize how easily they get distracted
  • Feel the impact of lost time and focus
  • Discover a healthier alternative

Our Goals

To make people aware of their screen habits and help them take back control of their focus.
